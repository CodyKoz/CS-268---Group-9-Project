Boyd Park
1202 Fairway St, Eau Claire, WI 54701

Boyd park offers a wide range of activities and provides access to the Eau Claire
River. Their is a bridge for pedestrians that crosses the river granting access
to trails on either side of the river. Not only are there hiking trails there is
shoreline fishing and in the winter you can visit a great outdoor skating rink.
The rink has hockey nets but also allows days for just ice skating, it is open
from 4 - 8pm during the week and 1 - 9pm during the weekends.

Boyd park in the summer offers nature trails with a parking lot close by. Picnic
areas and shelters are available as well as different types of recreational items
that can be used in the field. Boyd park spans over 7.2 acres and is a great place
to visit if you are looking for outdoor activities.
