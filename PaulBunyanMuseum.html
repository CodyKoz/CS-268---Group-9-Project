Paul Bunyan Logging Camp
Paul Bunyan Logging Camp, Eau Claire, WI 54703

The Paul Bunyan Logging Camp Museum is a historical sitespread over 300
acres. It offers a glimpse into the life of 19th-century loggers through
exhibits and a replica logging camp. Established in 1934, the museum found
its permanent home in Carson Park in 1972. Today, it continues to educate
visitors about Wisconsin's logging history and its impact on the nation.

Don't miss the chance to take a picture with the statues of Paul Bunyan and Babe the Blue Ox!
